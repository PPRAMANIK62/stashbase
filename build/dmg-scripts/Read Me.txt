StashBase Installation Guide

Recommended installation:
1. Drag StashBase.app into the Applications folder.
2. Open StashBase from Applications.

If macOS says StashBase is damaged, or asks to move it to Trash:
1. Return to this DMG window.
2. Right-click "Fix.sh".
3. Choose "Open With" -> "Terminal".
4. Enter your Mac password when prompted.
5. StashBase opens automatically after the repair finishes.
